The simple ideas that underlie our daily informal or formal thinking are worthy of some attention. Thinking about thinking is important both for 1) effective and better thinking from a practical point of view and 2) for satisfying the tendency of the mind to seek and discover structure, patterns, depth and conceptual beauty. Topics to be discussed include: symbols, structure, abstraction, analogy, modelling, meaning, ontology...etc.
